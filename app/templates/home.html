How to use

Click on the "Try it out!" button and it will ask you to upload an image. You may choose from a variety of planets in our solar system including, the sun, the moon and even asteroids! Once you have uploaded an image it will what you have put in. Enjoy!

Our Mission

Our mission is to help those that have very little knowledge or have forgotten a lot about our solar system. We are trying to help people learn more about space as we humans start traveling more outwards towards space and trying to make other planets living such as mars.
